Department of Ocean Engineering & Naval Architecture

The Department of Ocean Engineering and Naval Architecture was established in 1952, the first of its kind in the whole nation. In it's 69 years of service to the nation, it has made significant contributions to shipbuilding and shipping industries, including the Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard, DRDO and other allied organizations.
